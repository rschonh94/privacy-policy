Privacy Policy - Stack Rush

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Introduction

Stack Rush ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile game developed by Robin Schonhardt. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Stack Rush does not require any personal information to play. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Automatically Collected Information

Our app uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

  • Unity Analytics: We use Unity Analytics to understand how players interact with the game (e.g., levels played, session duration). This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Unity Ads: Our app displays advertisements through Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and general location data to serve relevant ads. For more information, see Unity's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: If you make in-app purchases, Google Play handles all payment processing. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

In-App Purchases

Stack Rush offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs, theme unlocks, ad removal). All purchases are processed securely through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment or financial information.

Advertising

Stack Rush displays ads through Unity Ads, including interstitial ads and optional rewarded video ads. You can opt to watch rewarded ads voluntarily to earn in-game currency. Interstitial ads can be permanently removed through an in-app purchase.

Children's Privacy

Stack Rush is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Storage

Game progress (scores, unlocked themes, coins) is stored locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app, this data will be deleted.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to the Google Play Store (e.g., for rating the app). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
